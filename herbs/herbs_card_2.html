Crown Flower

  • Synonym:
    ksheerparna, arkaparna
  • Salient features of identification :
    Plant : Tall Shrub Bark is furrowed, yellowish white, branches are stout
    Root : Stem : Leaf: Sessile, elliptic acute apex, thick glaucous green Flower : Purplish white Fruit : foliate thick, fleshy green
  • Part used:Whole plant
  • Dosage : : Root Bark powder 0.5-1 gm, flower powder1-2gm
  • Ayurvedic pharmacology – Rasa :Kashaya,Tikta Guna : Laghu,Rooksha Veerya:Ushna Vipaka: Katu
  • Karma –Doshaghna – Vatakaphashamaka
  • Others : Kushthar, gulamhar,udarhar,kanduhar
  • Modern pharmacology: stomachic,purgative,digestive,antihelminthic
  • Phytoconstituents :laurane,sacchrose,holarrhetine
  • Yoga : arkadikshaya, ark lavana arkadilchoorna
  • Reference : क्षीरमर्कस्य तिक्तोष्णं स्निग्धं सलवणं लघु ।कुष्ट गुल्मोदरहरं श्रेष्टमेतत् विरेचनम् ॥ ( भावप्रकाश )
