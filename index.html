About Us

We aim to create an innovative digital herbarium that offers a virtual garden tour, allowing users to explore interactive 3D models of plants and herbs.

This platform will enable users to zoom in and out and view plants from all angles, including detailed views of leaves, roots, and fruits. Each plant entry will include comprehensive information such as its botanical name, common name, Sanskrit name, medicinal uses, habitat, and more.

  • To enhance the learning experience, we will integrate high-quality multimedia elements, including images, videos, and audio descriptions. The platform will feature advanced search and filter options, enabling users to easily locate specific plants based on criteria like medicinal uses, region, and type.
  • Additionally, we will offer guided virtual tours that highlight specific themes, such as plants beneficial for digestive health, immunity, and skincare. User interaction will be a key component, with features that allow users to bookmark their favorite plants, take notes, and share information on social media.
  • The goal is to create an engaging, educational, and user-friendly platform that promotes the appreciation of plant diversity and medicinal value. By leveraging technology, we aim to increase accessibility to botanical knowledge and foster a deeper understanding of the vital role plants play in health and ecology. This project presents an opportunity to combine botany, technology, and user engagement in a meaningful way.

India is a treasure trove of herbal plants, it is, in a way our Green Gold.

Services

The Digital Herbarium will provide an immersive platform for users to explore plants through interactive 3D models with detailed views of leaves, roots, and fruits. Each entry will include botanical information, medicinal uses, and habitat details, enhanced with images, videos, and audio guides. Users can search and filter plants by various criteria, take virtual tours, and bookmark favorites. Community engagement features like forums and social media sharing will foster interaction.

Virtual Plant Exploration

3D Interactive Models.
Users can explore 3D models of plants, zoom in on specific parts like leaves, stems, roots, and fruits, and rotate them for a complete view.

Comprehensive Plant Database

Provide detailed descriptions, including botanical names, common names, and Sanskrit names. Medicinal Uses: Highlight plants’ medicinal benefits and traditional uses in healthcare. Filter/Search: Advanced search options to filter plants by region, medicinal use, plant type, etc.

Community Engagement

Create a platform where users can interact, share their findings, discuss plant care, and ask experts questions. Integrate features allowing users to share interesting plants, facts, or their favorite virtual tours on social media.Hold plant identification challenges or competitions to engage the community.

High-Quality Multimedia

Digital Herbarium includes high-resolution images, detailed videos, and audio guides for each plant. These elements enhance the user experience by offering visual and auditory learning, enabling users to explore plant features in-depth, and understand their medicinal uses, growth habits, and habitats interactively.

Mobile App

Provide an app with offline capabilities, allowing users to explore the herbarium from anywhere. Use location-based services to guide users to nearby plants or herbariums during nature walks.

Personalized User Experience

Bookmarking & Note-Taking:
Allow users to save their favorite plants or virtual tours, take personal notes, and track learning.
Recommendations & Reminders:
Suggest plants based on previous visits or user interests. User Profiles: Allow users to create profiles to save their learning history, bookmarks, and preferences.
